About us

Welcome to the largest nightclub in Kalmar here you can enjoy your drink in one of our three bars with unique themes or dance on one of our two dance floors. Here at Sandra, we offer a unique night out no matter what you enjoy. If you love to dance, there is always room on one of our dance floors, if you on the other hand prefer to sit and have a nice conversation you can sit in our slightly calmer bar.
Sandra got its start in 1972 and was founded by Torbjörn Håkansson who has almost 50 years of experience in the industry so we here at Sandra know how to make sure everyone gets the best night possible, no matter if it is a convention, party, or any other event planned.
